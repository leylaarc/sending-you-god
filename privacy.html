Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Sending You God ("we," "us," or "our") operates sendingyougod.com and sells handcrafted mini eco-wood crosses online. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you place an order, we collect information you provide directly, including:

  • For yourself: your name, email address, and shipping address
  • For gift orders: your email address, the recipient's name and shipping address, an optional gift message, and the name you'd like on the message card

Payment information (such as credit or debit card numbers) is collected and processed by Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers.

When you visit our site, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you view. We do not currently use analytics or advertising cookies on this site.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Ship your cross to you or your gift recipient
  • Send order confirmations and related communications
  • Respond to questions or support requests
  • Prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to run our business:

  • Stripe — to process payments securely
  • Shipping carriers — to deliver your order to the address you provide
  • Service providers — who help us operate the website or fulfill orders, under confidentiality obligations
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

Third-party services

We use Stripe for payment processing and Google Fonts to display typography on our site. When you load our pages, Google may receive technical data such as your IP address. Please review their privacy policies for details on how they handle data.

Data retention

We keep order-related information for as long as needed to fulfill your order, handle returns or disputes, and meet tax, accounting, and legal requirements. Payment and transaction records are also retained by Stripe according to their policies.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including using secure payment processing through Stripe. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or to opt out of certain data uses. To make a request, contact us using the email below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion, subject to certain exceptions.

Children

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at hello@sendingyougod.com.

This policy is provided for informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice.
